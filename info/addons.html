Raiding Addons and Suggestions

These suggestions will be split into multiple sections. Required addons are shown by the *.

User Interface Addons

At League of Assassins we believe that in order to play at one's full potential a modification from the standard user interface is required. This is due to the fact that it opens up a lot more space on the screen and allows one to personalise their experience to suit their playstyle. The following are the recommended User Interface Addons:

ElvUI

ElvUI is a revolutionary addon that completely replaces that standard user interface and offers many benefits. It is used by nearly all of the core raid team and is the recommended user interface.

WeakAuras2

WeakAuras 2 is an addons that allows you to visually keep track of the status of your character, whether it be buffs, ability availability, cooldowns, debuffs or anything else. It is fairly complex to use so it is recommended that if you do not understand how to work it fully that you import configurations that others have made via strings. There are a few strings linked below that may be of use, or at least a starting point.

Raid Encounter Assistant Addons

Deadly Boss Mods (DBM)*

DBM is a must-have for all raid encounters. It provides the raid with timers of boss abilities and alerts when important things happen.

GTFO

The functionality of this addon is simple and obviously derived from its name. It simply tells you to "GTFO" via a horrible noise when you are standing in harmfell AOE effects (AKA. "Shit").

Iskar Assist*

This addon makes passing the Eye of Anzu, and dispelling players, VERY easy by providing a small in-game party UI on which you left-click on a player to pass the Eye to them, or right-click them to dispell.

Raeli's Spell Announcer (RSA)

RSA is another addon that's function is easily deduced from it's title. It announces to the instance when certain spells are casted. This is recommended for Tanks and Healers as it allows the entire raid to know when cooldowns are used. It is highly customisable and any spell can be set to be announced.

Skada

This is the guild-preferred damage and healing meter addon. It allows you to see a variety of statistics about fights including DPS, DTPS, HPS, Damage Done and Healing Done.

General Useful Addons

Ask Mr Robot*

This addon is required as it allows the Raid Leader to quickly use AskMrRobot to check what gear each individual has and if a drop is an upgrade for them.
